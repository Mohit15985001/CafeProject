About Us

Durga café was founded in 2019 by four budding entrepreneurs: Pranjal, Mohit, Raj and Mridul. We quickly became India’s number one café within the first three months of opening due to our exquisite cuisine, high quality service and hygiene, and most importantly our highly affordable prices. Our café saw great growth before the COVID pandemic hit.

During the pandemic, we gave back to the society by distributing essential amenities to the needy. Post pandemic we are again seeing immense growth as people are flooding back in to enjoy the luxurious café experience at our cafés. We have opened many new outlets post pandemic.
